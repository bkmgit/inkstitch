First of all a very big thank you for all the reports we received from  Ink/Stitch version 3.1.0beta testers.

We received lots of reports and had to fix lots of issues. 

We are therefore releasing Ink/Stitch version 3.1.0beta2.

Once more we need you to test to make sure that we did not introduce new issues.

Test reports are much appreciated on <https://github.com/inkstitch/inkstitch/issues/new>

See a [full list of updates, bug fixes and new features](/upcoming)

Download the version for your operating system and follow the installation instructions using that file:

  * [Installation Instructions](/docs/install-windows/)
* macOS
